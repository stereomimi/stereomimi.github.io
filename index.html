MZS Neogeo Audio Driver

An audio driver for the NeoGeo developed in Z80 assembly.
It handles music and SFX playback, supporting FM, SSG and ADPCM-A channels.
It's used in various games and homebrew projects, such as Project Neon!
The newest 2.0 alpha branch is in development!
